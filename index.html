Welcome to jrpalma.com

Here you will find simple tips, tutorials, and news about software engineering and technology. This site's goal is to share simple, useful, and practical information.

How is this site different?

Have you ever traversed the web for hours to just to piece something together? You are not alone. Simple solutions to problems can be scattered across multiple sites on the web. Contrary to most sites on the web, this site is focused on providing comprehensive solutions in a simple way.

No tracking, no selling, no comments

This site does not store information about its visitors. We all have a lot of things to do in our daily lives. Comment moderation should not be one of them unless you are being paid for it. If you would like to comment or send corrections, you can visit the Github Pages and open an issue there.
